My Favorite Music Group: 702

702 is a popular R&B girl group from the late 1990s known for their smooth vocals and catchy songs. One of their biggest hits is "Where My Girls At?", which became an anthem for female empowerment. I enjoy their music because it has a strong vibe, great harmonies, and timeless style that still sounds good today.
